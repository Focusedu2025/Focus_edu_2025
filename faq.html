Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What age group do you cater to?
We specialize in early childhood education for children aged 1 to 6 years old, the most critical period for brain development.
What is Whole-Brain Development?
Whole-Brain Development involves nurturing both left and right brain functions — logic, creativity, memory, emotional intelligence, and more — to create well-rounded learners.
How is BCI (Brain-Computer Interface) used in your program?
We use safe, non-invasive BCI headsets to monitor brain activity and personalize learning experiences based on each child’s cognitive patterns.
Do children enjoy the classes?
Absolutely! Our sessions are interactive, playful, and designed to stimulate young minds while keeping them engaged and happy.
How often should my child attend classes?
We recommend 1–2 sessions per week for consistency and maximum benefit. Our staff can help tailor a schedule that fits your child’s needs.
Is there a trial session?
Yes! We offer a free trial class so you and your child can experience our unique approach first-hand before committing.
